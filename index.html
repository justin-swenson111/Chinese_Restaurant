Main Dishes

Pad Thai: A stir fried rice-noodle dish. It is made with rice noodles, shrimp, peanuts, scrambled eggs, and bean sprouts.

Tom Yum Goong: A hot and sour soup made with Broth, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, galangal, lime juice, fish sauce, and chili peppers

Gaeng Daeng: Red curry cooked in cocunut milk with chicken, beef, pork, duck, or shrimp added in

Beverages

Thai Iced Coffee: coffee made with a very dark roast mixed with condensed milk and evaporated milk

Thai Lime Iced Tea: a strongly brewed black tea mixed and tamarind, and spices such as star anise and cardamom and mixed with lime juice. lime juice can be substituted for lemon, and honey can be added for sweetness

Lemongrass Tea: tea steeped with lemongrass and ginger, with honey andemon mixed in

Appetizers

Shrimp Lettuce Wraps: shrimp avocado and julienned cucumbers wrapped in lettuce, garnished in cilantro, lime, green onions, and peanuts

Spring Rolls: Pork, carrot, cabbage, and noodles wrapped with rice flour then deep fried

Yam Naem Khao Thot: fried rice balls coated in thai curry served with a salad made of many different herps

Desserts

Khanom Bueang: made with meringue and either sweet or salty toppings of your choice, such as shredded coconut, Foi Thong, or chopped scallions, wrapped in a rice flout pancake

Tub Tim Grob: water chestnurs soaked in grenadine that are boiled in tapioca flour

Thai Banana Roti: Roti dough wrapped around banana slices with a maly condensed milk dirxxle on the outside, other fillings can be requested such as scrambled egg, apples, or chocolate sauce
